Justin Plante | ABOUT

Justin is a member of the International Coaching Federation (ICF) and had studied coaching from some of the most gifted leaders and coaches in the industry through Dream Releaser Coaching.

Justin is also an experienced executive in the commercial insurance industry where he has served for 23 years in a variety of roles. Some of these include COO, EVP, SVP and in various national leadership roles for large insurance brokers and carriers. He also is served as volunteer soccer coach for 20 years.

Justin had his MBA from Liberty University and specializes in executive coaching, business coaching, non-profit coaching, leadership development and personal development.
He serves on the board of a non profit organization helping troubled teens and serves in his local church along with his wife Christine. He has 4 kids and 6 grandchildren and lives in the St Louis area
